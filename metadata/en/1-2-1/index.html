    Indicator 1.2.1. Number of people paying social insurance, health insurance and unemployment insurance

    June 2021

    Vietnam Social Security

    No. 7 Trang Thi, Hoan Kiem District, Hanoi City

    bhxh@vss.gov.vn

    a) Number of people paying social insurance

    Number of people paying social insurance includes the number of people participating in compulsory social insurance premiums and the number of people paying voluntary social insurance premiums.

    Compulsory social insurance is a type of social insurance organized by the State that employees and employers must participate in.

    Voluntary social insurance is a type of social insurance organized by the State in which participants can choose a payment level and payment method suitable to their income and the State has a policy to support insurance premiums. social insurance for participants to enjoy retirement and survivorship benefits.

    The subjects participating in compulsory social insurance are prescribed by the Law on Social Insurance.

    Voluntary social insurance participants are Vietnamese citizens aged full 15 years and older and are not subject to compulsory social insurance payment.

    b) Number of people paying health insurance

    Health insurance is a form of compulsory insurance that is applied to subjects under the Law on Health Insurance for health care, not for profit and organized by the State.

    Participants in health insurance are regulated by the Law on Health Insurance.

    c) Number of people paying unemployment insurance

    Unemployment insurance is a regime aimed at compensating a part of an employee's income when he or she loses a job, supporting workers in vocational training, job maintenance, and job search on the basis of contributions to the Self-Insurance Fund. wish.

    The subjects participating in unemployment insurance are regulated under the Employment Law.

    National level statistical reporting mode

    The national statistical reporting regime was issued under Decree No. 60/2018/ND-CP dated April 20, 2018. According to regulations, Vietnam Social Insurance collects the form "00.1N/BCB-BHXH - Number of people paying social insurance, unemployment insurance, health insurance". The report is calculated starting from January 1 to the end of December 31. Periodically, Vietnam Social Insurance aggregates data from Social Security of provinces/cities directly under the Central Government and sends it to the General. Department of Statistic. Then, the General Statistics Office appraises the data according to the provisions of the Statistics Law and publishes the data on "Number of people paying social insurance, health insurance and unemployment insurance".

    The global set of sustainable development indicators uses the indicator “1.3.1. Proportion of population covered by social protection floors/systems, by sex, distinguishing children, unemployed persons, older persons, persons with disabilities, pregnant women, newborns, work-injury victims and the poor and the vulnerable”. However, due to the characteristics of Vietnam, there is no clear concept of the term "social protection". Social protection is close to the concept of social assistance, which is one of the three basic pillars of the welfare system. With the purpose of overcoming risks, social assistance together with social insurance have the function of reducing risks, and proactive labor market policies to prevent risks for people. Therefore, to replace the global indicator 1.3.1, Vietnam uses the indicator "Number of people paying social insurance, health insurance, unemployment insurance" and some related indicators of Vietnam. like:

    1.2.2. Number of people entitled to social insurance, health insurance and unemployment insurance

    1.2.3. Number of people receiving monthly social support in the community.

    1.2.4. Number of people receiving unexpected social support

    1.2.5. The number of people being raised is concentrated in social protection establishments and social houses.
