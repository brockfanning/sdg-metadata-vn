    Goal 4: Ensure quality, equitable, inclusive education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all

    Target 4.1: By 2030, ensure that all girls and boys complete free, equitable and quality primary and lower secondary education (Global Goal 4.1)

    Indicator 4.1.2. Percentage of students who are recognized to complete the primary program

    June 2021

    Ministry of Education and Training

    No. 35 Dai Co Viet, Hanoi

    bogddt@moet.gov.vn

    The percentage of students who have been recognized for completing elementary school is the percentage of students who are recognized to have completed elementary school compared to the number of students who entered 5th grade at the end of that school year.

    The list of administrative units in Vietnam issued together with the Prime Minister's Decision No. 124/2004/QD-TTg dated July 8, 2004 and the changes updated by the General Statistics Office to date December 31, 2019)

    Statistical reporting mode issued by the Ministry of Education and Training.

    - Number of students recognized to complete the primary school program in school year t: Compiled from form 02-TH-CN, the Education and Training sector statistical reporting mode

    - Number of students in grade 5 at the end of the school year t: Compiled from the form 02-TH-DN, the Education and Training sector statistical reporting mode

    - Periodically, the Departments of Education and Training of the provinces/cities directly under the Central Government collect information on each form and send it to the Ministry of Education and Training for calculation purposes.

    Year

    Ministry of Education and Training

    Ministry of Education and Training

    The percentage of students who are recognized to complete primary school is an indicator of a school system's ability to retain students; indirectly reflect the dropout rate. Percentage of accredited students who complete elementary education measures the sustainability of literacy.

    Method of computation

    General data and disaggregated data for 2019.

    (Published source: 2020 national report on 5-year progress towards implementation of sustainable development goals. Data from the Ministry of Education and Training)

    The indicator "Percentage of students recognized to complete primary school" is one of Vietnam's indicators to reflect the global sustainable development target " 4.1.1: Proportion of children and young people: (a) in grades 2/3; (b) at the end of primary; and (c) at the end of lower secondary achieving at least a minimum proficiency level in (i) reading and (ii) mathematics, by sex”

    However, the indicator does not guarantee global comparability because the content, scope and calculation method of Vietnam are not the same as the global standard.

    - Circular No. 03/2019/TT-BKHĐT dated January 22, 2019 stipulating the set of statistical indicators for sustainable development of Vietnam;

    - Circular No. 24/2018/TT-BGDĐT dated September 28, 2018 of the Ministry of Education and Training on promulgating the reporting regime for statistics of the Education and Training sector.
