    Goal 4: Ensure quality, equitable, inclusive education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all

    Target 4.7: By 2030, ensure that all learners are equipped with the knowledge and skills needed to promote sustainable development (Global Goal 4.7)

    Indicator 4.7.1. Percentage of teachers with training qualifications and above

    June 2021

    Ministry of Education and Training

    No. 35 Dai Co Viet, Hanoi

    bogddt@moet.gov.vn

    Percentage of teachers meeting training standards or higher is the percentage of the number of teachers meeting training standards or higher by grade level (kindergarten, primary, middle and high school) compared to the total number of teachers of the respective grade level.

    A trained teacher means a teacher who has been trained and graduated from a pedagogical school with the corresponding qualifications as prescribed.

    The list of administrative units in Vietnam issued together with the Prime Minister's Decision No. 124/2004/QD-TTg dated July 8, 2004 and the changes updated by the General Statistics Office to date December 31, 2019)

    Statistical reporting mode issued by the Ministry of Education and Training.

    The Departments of Education and Training of the provinces/cities directly under the Central Government report the data according to the form issued by the Ministry of Education and Training. Based on data from the Departments, the Ministry of Education and Training compiles data of the indicator.

    Year

    Ministry of Education and Training

    Ministry of Education and Training

    Teachers play an important role in ensuring the size and quality of education. All teachers should have adequate, relevant, relevant pedagogical training to teach at their chosen grade level and be academically qualified in the subjects they wish to teach. This indicator evaluates the percentage of teachers who are well-trained in pedagogy in order to improve teaching quality.

    Method of computation

    Annual data from 2016-2019 by grade level.

    (Published source: Data on the website of the Ministry of Education and Training).

    This indicator reflects the global target “ 4.c.1: Proportion of teachers in: (a) pre-primary; (b) primary; (c) lower secondary; and (d) upper secondary education who have received at least the minimum organized teacher training (e.g. pedagogical training) pre-service or in-service required for teaching at the relevant level in a given country”. Globally, in addition to disaggregated by sex and education level (similar to Vietnam's), it is also classified by type of organization (public/private).

    - Circular No. 03/2019/TT-BKHĐT dated January 22, 2019 stipulating the set of statistical indicators for sustainable development of Vietnam;
