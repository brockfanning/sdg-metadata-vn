    Indicator: 12.2.3

    Download PDF Download SDMX

    Objective 12: Ensure a sustainable production and consumption model

    Target 12.2: By 2030, achieve sustainable management and efficient use of natural resources; rational exploitation and economical and sustainable use of mineral resources (global target 12.2)

    Indicator 12.2.3. The ratio of the area of Vietnam's seas and islands is measured from, the gravity ratio is 1:250,000

    June 2021

    Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment

    No. 10 Ton That Thuyet - Hanoi

    portal@monre.gov.vn

    The ratio of the area of Vietnam's seas and islands measured from, the gravity ratio
    1:250,000 is the percentage of the area of Vietnam's seas and islands measured from, the gravity ratio 1:250,000 compared with the total area of sea and islands of Vietnam.

    Administrative data from the Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment

    5 years.

    Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment

    Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment

    The indicator serves the planning of geological baseline survey of minerals, land use planning, traffic planning, construction planning, socio-economic development planning of the country.

    Method of computation

    Only figures for 2020

    (Published source: 2020 national report on 5-year progress towards implementation of sustainable development goals. Data from the Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment)

    This indicator reflects Vietnam's sustainable development goals (target 12.2). The set of global sustainable development indicators does not have this indicator

    - Circular No. 03/2019/TT-BKHDT dated January 22, 2019 stipulating the set of statistical indicators for sustainable development of Vietnam;
